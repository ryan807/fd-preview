How it works

From booking to a complete, risk-screened profile.

  • 1. Patient receives a personalized intake link by SMS or email after scheduling.
  • 2. AI guides them through assessments, history, and document upload conversationally.
  • 3. Responses are analyzed for completeness, risk factors, and clinical flags.
  • 4. A complete patient profile is waiting in your EHR before the first appointment.
