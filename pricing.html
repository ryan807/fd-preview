Pricing FAQ

Common questions.

Is there really a free trial with no card?

Yes. The Free Trial includes 100 conversations and a full BAA from day one, with no credit card required. It's the easiest way to see Front Door work on your own inquiries.

What counts as a "conversation"?

A conversation is a single patient engagement — a chat, call, or intake session — regardless of length. Overages are billed at the per-conversation rate listed on your plan.

Can I start with just the Virtual Assistant?

Yes. The Free Trial runs the Virtual Assistant alone. Clinical Intake Automation is included starting on the Professional plan, and the two work together seamlessly once enabled.

What does Enterprise pricing depend on?

Enterprise is scoped to your number of facilities, channels, conversation volume, integrations, and support needs. Book a demo and we'll walk through a written estimate.
